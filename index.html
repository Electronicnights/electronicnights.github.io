Electronic Night Nr.6 & 7

We received 182 works from 19 countries, with many submissions of outstanding quality. Due to limited performance slots for Electronic Nights 6 and 7, we have selected the following pieces. The performances will take place on February 4 and March 4, 2026, at the School of Music, Texas Tech University.

If additional works are programmed in future Electronic Nights events, we will announce them as well.

Electronic Night Nr. 6:

  • Accretion Shock (2025), Composer: Chris Arrell
  • How to Tear Yourself Apart (2024), Composer: Zac Dulkin
  • Unformation (2023), Composer: Erik Nyström
  • Fleeting Experience (2025), Composer: Clemens von Reusner

Electronic Night Nr. 7:

  • Radjutan (2025), Composer: Candra Bangun Setyawan
  • Can a Canaanite Put a Qanun in a Canon? (2025), Composer: Sami Seif
  • Wander (2025), Composer: Ophelia Badretdinova
  • This Is Only a Test (2025), Composer: David Jason Snow
  • An Insect that Shuns the Light (2023), Composer: Gordon Delap
  • Un Dado Roído (2023), Composer: Lucas Fuentes Vergara
